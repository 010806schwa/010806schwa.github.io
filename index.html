About

Hello, I am Haotian Lin.

I am a master's student in Communication Engineering at the School of Informatics, Xiamen University, affiliated with the Key Laboratory of Underwater Acoustic Communication and Marine Information Technology, Ministry of Education.

My research focuses on deep learning and generative models for underwater acoustic signals, especially robust recognition under low signal-to-noise conditions and open-set recognition for unknown categories.
